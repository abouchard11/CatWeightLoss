Cat Weight Tracker

Privacy Policy

Your cat's health data stays on your device. We believe privacy is a fundamental right.

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cat Weight Tracker ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our iOS application.

All personal data stays on your device

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User-Provided Data

The App allows you to track your cat's weight loss journey by recording:

  • Weight measurements
  • Feeding schedules
  • Activity logs
  • Photos (stored locally on your device)
  • Notes and observations

Important: All user-provided data is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit, store, or access this data on any remote server.

1.2 Automatically Collected Data

We collect limited, anonymized analytics data through Firebase Analytics:

  • Anonymous device identifier (assigned by Firebase)
  • Device type and iOS version
  • App usage patterns (screens viewed, features used)
  • Performance metrics (app crashes, load times)
  • General location (country/region level only, no precise location)

No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is collected. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location data, or any information that could identify you personally.

1.3 QR Code Activation

When you activate the App using a QR code:

  • An anonymous activation token is processed locally
  • The token associates your app instance with a specific pet food brand partnership
  • No personal information is transmitted during activation

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Local Data Usage

Data you enter (weight, feeding schedules, etc.) is used solely to:

  • Display your cat's progress within the App
  • Generate charts and insights on your device
  • Provide feeding recommendations based on your inputs

2.2 Analytics Usage

Anonymous analytics data is used to:

  • Understand how users interact with the App
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Aggregate usage statistics for white-label partners

Analytics data cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All personal data (weight entries, photos, notes) is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure SwiftData framework. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is protected by iOS sandboxing
  • Is encrypted with your device's hardware encryption
  • Is deleted when you delete the App

3.2 No Cloud Backup

We do not provide cloud backup or sync services. If you:

  • Delete the App, all local data is permanently deleted
  • Switch devices, data does not transfer automatically
  • Users are responsible for backing up their device via iCloud or iTunes if desired

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data. Firebase may:

  • Assign an anonymous identifier to your app instance
  • Collect device and usage information as described above
  • Store analytics data on Google's servers

Firebase's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4.2 No Other Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or disclose any data to third parties except as required by law.

5. Your Data Rights

5.1 Access and Deletion

Access

All your data is accessible within the App at any time.

Deletion

Delete individual entries within the App, or uninstall the App to remove all data.

Control

You have complete control over what data you enter and retain.

5.2 Analytics Opt-Out

You can limit analytics collection through your iOS device settings:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements
  • Disable "Share iPhone Analytics" to limit system-level analytics
  • Note: This is a device-wide setting and affects all apps

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. The App is designed for adult pet owners to track their cat's health information.

7. International Users and Data Transfers

7.1 GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA):

  • Legal Basis: We process data based on your consent (by using the App) and legitimate interests (improving the App)
  • Data Minimization: We collect only essential anonymized data
  • Right to Access: You can view all your data within the App
  • Right to Erasure: Delete the App to remove all local data
  • Right to Object: Disable analytics through iOS settings
  • Data Transfers: Analytics data may be transferred to Google servers in the US under standard contractual clauses

7.2 CCPA Compliance (California Users)

If you are a California resident:

  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell personal information
  • Categories Collected: Anonymous device identifiers and usage analytics only
  • Purpose: App improvement and performance monitoring
  • Right to Delete: Uninstall the App to delete all local data
  • Right to Know: This policy describes all data collection practices

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted within the App and on our website. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App
  • Analytics Data: Retained by Firebase for up to 14 months, then automatically deleted
  • Activation Tokens: Processed at activation only, not stored long-term

10. Security Measures

We implement security measures including:

  • iOS sandboxing and hardware encryption for local data
  • HTTPS encryption for analytics transmission
  • No storage of sensitive data on remote servers
  • Anonymous identifiers only (no PII)

11. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or data practices, please contact us.

For white-label partners and enterprise inquiries, please contact your brand representative or our business development team.

Your Privacy Matters: We are committed to transparency and protecting your privacy. This App is designed to keep your data on your device, under your control, at all times.
