## 1. Purpose

This Letter of Intent outlines the terms for a 90-day pilot program in which Provider will deliver a white-label iOS application to Brand for the purpose of customer engagement and reorder attribution.

Both parties intend to evaluate the technical, operational, and commercial viability of the solution during the pilot period.

## 2. Pilot Scope

### 2.1 Duration
- **Start Date:** [START_DATE]
- **End Date:** 90 days from start ([END_DATE])
- Either party may terminate with 14 days written notice

### 2.2 Product Scope
- White-label iOS application branded as [BRAND_NAME]
- QR code activation via [NUMBER] SKU(s): [LIST_SKUS]
- Core features: Pet profile creation, weight tracking, reorder reminders, affiliate link integration

### 2.3 Brand Deliverables
- Brand assets (logo, colors, fonts, brand guidelines) within 7 days of LOI signature
- Affiliate links for reorder functionality
- QR codes placed on packaging for designated SKUs
- Minimum target: [MIN_ACTIVATIONS] app activations during pilot period

### 2.4 Provider Deliverables
- iOS app submitted to Apple App Store within 21 days of receiving brand assets
- Backend infrastructure for user management and analytics
- Weekly reporting on activation and engagement metrics
- Bi-weekly check-in calls with Brand stakeholder

## 5. Data Ownership & Privacy

### 5.1 Customer Data Ownership
- Brand owns all customer data collected via the application
- Provider acts as data processor on Brand's behalf
- Provider will export and deliver all customer data to Brand at pilot conclusion

### 5.2 Aggregated Data
- Provider may use aggregated, anonymized data for product improvement
- Provider may reference Brand as a customer (with approval) in marketing materials post-pilot

### 5.3 Data Security
- Provider will implement industry-standard security practices (encryption, secure authentication)
- Provider will comply with applicable privacy laws (CCPA, GDPR where applicable)
- Provider will not sell or share customer data with third parties without Brand consent

---

## 6. Intellectual Property

### 6.1 Brand Assets
- Brand grants Provider limited license to use Brand's logos, trademarks, and brand assets solely for the pilot application
- License terminates upon pilot conclusion or termination

### 6.2 Application Code
- Provider retains all intellectual property rights to the application platform and codebase
- Brand receives no source code or IP rights during pilot

### 6.3 White-Label Customization
- Brand-specific customizations (UI, branding) remain Provider's property but are exclusive to Brand during pilot period

---

Upon successful completion of pilot (achieving success metrics in Section 4), parties agree to negotiate in good faith toward a long-term agreement with the following framework:

### 7.1 Ongoing Pricing Options

**Option B: Monthly SaaS Model**
- Monthly platform fee: $[AMOUNT]/month
- Includes [NUMBER] activations per month

## 8. Termination & Wind-Down

### 8.1 Early Termination
Either party may terminate this LOI with 14 days written notice for any reason.

### 8.2 Wind-Down Obligations
Upon termination:
- Brand will pay for all activations achieved through termination date
- Provider will deliver all customer data to Brand within 14 days
- Provider will remove app from Apple App Store within 30 days
- Brand will cease use of application and Provider's IP

### 8.3 No Ongoing Obligations
Neither party has ongoing obligations post-termination beyond wind-down activities above.
